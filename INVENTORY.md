# Science Room Inventory — Cleaned Planning View

*De-duplicated, de-noised inventory derived from the May 2026 photo survey (174 photos, Gemini 3.1 Flash). The raw per-photo AI output is preserved in `science_room_inventory.md` / `full_inventory.csv` — consult those for original wording, low-confidence flags, and per-photo context.*

**What changed from the raw file:** collapsed duplicate sightings (same item seen across multiple photos), dropped pure noise (label fragments, packaging text, "partially visible unknown bottle"), merged the 9 raw buckets into a cleaner spine, and tagged each line with the **subject(s)** it serves so the room can be planned across all courses, not just Biology.

**Counts are photo-derived estimates** — good enough for "do we have a class set?" planning, not an audited asset count. Verify before ordering.
