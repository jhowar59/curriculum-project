#### SECTION 3 Projectile Motion

#### Neglecting air resistance, a projectile has a constant horizontal velocity and a constant downward free-fall acceleration.

In the absence of air resistance, projectiles follow a parabolic path.

#### **KEY TERM**

projectile motion
