#### SECTION 3 Conservation of Energy KEY TERM

- Energy can change form but can never be created or destroyed.
- Mechanical energy is the total kinetic and potential energy present in a given situation.
- In the absence of friction, mechanical energy is conserved, so the amount of mechanical energy remains constant.
